The Price of Life

Even when the correlation between poverty and number of people who cannot pay for a doctor appointment was expected, the importance of this data is more than just statistical, revealing that the health is not guaranteed for those living below the poverty line in this country. A deeper analysis should be conducted to bring to the table a discussion about the high cost of health care in the US in comparison with other countries and the social issues that might be attached to this situation.
